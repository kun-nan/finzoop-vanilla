Privacy Policy

We at Finzoop value your privacy and comply strictly with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act of 2023.

1. Data Collection

We only collect data necessitated to fulfill loan applications, insurance policies, or investment accounts. This includes KYC data securely passed to partners.

2. Data Usage

Your data is never sold to third parties. We use your data purely to compare and offer financial products as per your mandate.

3. Your Rights

As per the DPDP Act, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your digital personal data stored on our servers.
